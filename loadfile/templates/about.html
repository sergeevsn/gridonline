This web service extracts 3D cdp grid parameters from the text-file table or SEG-Y file. The table must contain columns: inline, crossline, X, Y. Columns must be space separated. SEG-Y trace headers must contain inlines, crosslines, X and Y in traditional byte positions (181 for X, 185 for Y, 189 for inline, 193 for crossline).

Grid parameters you get as a result:

  • - Min and max of inlines, crosslines, X and Y
  • - Formulas to get inlines and crosslines from X and Y and vice versa
  • - Coordinates of grid origin (inline 1, crossline 1)
  • - Grid azimuth in degrees
  • - Bin size (in consideration of square bin)
  • - Are inlines increase along Y axe or not (True or False)

You can calculate X, Y from given inline and crossline.

As well as, calculate inline and crossline from given X and Y
